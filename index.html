First History

Our story began back in 2021, specifically on June 21, when we started dating. That day, I was over the moon, because I had been in love with this girl for almost a year, but she had refused to get into a relationship—until suddenly everything changed.

It all started on the volleyball court near my house, because that’s where we began spending a lot of time together, and I started making a move and giving this girl an incredible amount of attention

The story of our first date

It all started on the roof with the 500 hryvnias I borrowed from my mom and dad. On our date, I stole a bottle of champagne from the home minibar and ordered sushi that cost 500 hryvnias—exactly what my mom had given me. I grabbed a blanket and two glasses, and we headed up to the roof of our nine-story building

Whatever you say, it was the best first date
